Join the Lake Elmo Chamber of Commerce

Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership

Price: Free!

Have your business listed in the Lake Elmo Chamber online directory.

Bronze Membership

Price: $1000 per year

Businesses get a 10% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce Gathering Room for any functions they would like to hold, as well as one spotlight on the Chamber of Commerce website per year.

Silver Membership

Price: $2500 per year

Businesses get a 15% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce Gathering Room for functions, and two spotlights on the Chamber of Commerce website per year, with your choice of what month your business is featured!

Gold Membership

Price: $6000 per year

Businesses selecting the Gold Membership with the Chamber of Commerce will be included in our executive level discussions, get use of the Gathering Room at a %25 discount and three spotlights, with the member's choice of what months the spotlight is featured!

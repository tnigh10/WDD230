Summary

Lake Elmo is conveniently situated close to the 694/494 beltway for easy access to the entire metro area. With just 6500 residents and a large mix of farms and multi-acre residential properties, it is a great blend of rural right next to larger cities. Take a look at what makes Lake Elmo special.

Just 5 minutes from downtown St. Paul, and 6 minutes to the beautiful St. Croix River.

The perfect blend of rural community near enough to the metropolitan are to be useful.

The Lake Elmo Park reserve is central to the community with facilities for boating, swimming, camping and hiking.

Most residents have 1-5 acres of land, but parks and farms of all sizes are common here.

We are no stranger to winter fun.

We make ice mazes in the winter and corn mazes in the summer and fall.

Nordic blood runs deep in our veins...and we have been known to do a few crazy things. (By the way, nothing is better than a sauna after an ice plunge. Great for your health too.)

And it just so happens, that one of the areas pre-eminent restaurants is located in our midst. Fabulous food. Notoriously good Sunday brunch.

Welcome to our community.
